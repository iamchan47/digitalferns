Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

At Digital Ferns, accessible from https://digitalferns.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Digital Ferns and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a contact or proposal request form on our website, we collect personal information you provide, including Name, Company Name, Work Email Address, Phone Number, Website URL, and Budget details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to conduct website rendering speed checkouts, search index audits, and competitor analyses. We also communicate proposals and contact information with your team.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Digital Ferns uses standard cookies to manage light/dark theme context toggling in browser local storage and monitors site traffic statistics using anonymous analytics page logs.
